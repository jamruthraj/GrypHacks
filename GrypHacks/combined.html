One Stop to Refresh Your Mind

Non-Addictive

None of the games are addictive and ensure you go back to work after a short break

Fun-Filled

Each of them is designed in such a way that in a short time you will have loads of fun

Innovative

Each of the games was built from scratch, we're open to feedback to make our games and this website better

The Intention

Studies have found that children ages 12 to 17 who are frequently bored are 50 percent more likely to smoke, drink, get drunk or use illegal drugs. Our goal is to make a website that helps people to take a small break between long hours of working or coding in a non-addictive but fun manner.

The Games
